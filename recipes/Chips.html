Chips

Description

This chips recipe is quick and simple which serves up to 2-4 people

This recipe requires...

  • Chips (obviously)
  • an oven
  • literally thats it

The instructions are as follows...

  1. Put chips on oven tray
  2. Preheat oven to 200 degrees celcius and stand there like an idiot
  3. Stick hand fully inside to determine if hot enough
  4. Once hot enough out tray in and set timer for 18 minutes
  5. Flip halfway
  6. After 18 minutes take tray out
