Pasta

Description

This pasta recipe is quick and simple which serves up to 2-4 people

This recipe requires...

  • pasta (obviously)
  • an STOVE
  • thats it

The instructions are as follows...

  1. Put 200g dry pasta in pot
  2. Turn stove on max heat
  3. Press hand firmly on stove surface to ensure it is hot AF
  4. Put timer on for 16 minutes
  5. Pretend to maintain pasta by stirring clockwise every few minutes so your mother doesnt think you forgot about the food smh
  6. After 16 minutes turn off stove and sieve pasta under cold water
  7. Add pasta back into stove and add a sauce of your choise YES YOUR CHOISE BRO OMG
  8. Turn on stove to half heat and stir sauce into pasta thoroughly
  9. Once fully mixed turn off stove and serve in bowlz
  10. Enjoy bru
